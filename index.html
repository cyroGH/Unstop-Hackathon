How DataFlow Works

Transform raw data, spreadsheets, and document scripts into professional video presentations in three simple steps.

01

Upload Documents

Drag and drop your Excel sheets, Word reports, or PDFs directly into our studio. Our AI layout parser scans the structure, text columns, and tables.

02

AI Storyboarding

Our neural engine automatically translates data summaries into high-impact scripts, maps out presentation slides, and designs custom 3D visualizations.

03

Narrate & Render

Select an AI narrator voice. DataFlow automatically compiles the visual transitions, records the voiceover, and renders your MP4 export at 3x speed.
